Interested in going to college in the United States? Then join us this summer for a 4-week accelerated course that will help you prepare, as an International student, for success in the US Higher Education system. Mornings will be spent building college and career-ready academic skills. You will: write a strong Personal Statement required for application to elite colleges and universities; engage in challenging projects that will prepare you for college-level research and writing; be immersed in US social practices that will improve your communication and relationships with Professors and potential employers.

Afternoons are about your social development. You will do community service, outreach project, fieldtrip, group project, homework, etc. Berkeley has a diverse culture and values your community engagement.
