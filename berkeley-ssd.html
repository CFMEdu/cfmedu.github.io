Program Value

The residential program is operated by CFM Educational Services, a partner of UC Berkeleyâ€™s Graduate School of Education and Lawrence Hall of Science. CFM has accumulated extensive experience in providing credible international education and reliable student services since 1991 with many U.S. universities and organizations.
Berkeley is an ethnically and culturally diverse campus offering a great summer opportunity to cultivate independent learning.
Residential counselors are Berkeley senior students and will provide academic advising, cultural guidance, social learning, and safety supervision.
The standard college dorm with integrated environment for college life, community, learning, and recreation ensures safety, security, and comfort.
A thoughtful meal plan gives students flexibility to go for their choices of multicultural restaurants and/or economically prepare their own foods in addition to the standard canteen dining.
The program organizes high-value excursions to let students enjoy California outdoors and cultural activities that are educational, enriching, friendship-building, and fun.

Housing

  • Standard college dormitory (5-minute walk to campus) with 24-hour monitored security system and keyed lobby entrance, elevator, and room door
  • Double or triple occupancy
  • Shared floor bathrooms and building kitchen appliances
  • WiFi throughout campus & dorms
  • Conveniently located across the street from campus and a few steps away from restaurants & shops

Meals

  • The program includes university meal plan with variety and multicultural options. Students can gather to eat, chat, and hang out, in the meantime enjoy the living/learning atmosphere.
  • Students have their flexibility to decide how and what they eat for weekday breakfast and weekend meals. Restaurants of different varieties are conveniently located around the campus and nearby the apartment that are very accessible.
  • Students can also prepare their own breakfast and midnight snack utilizing the refrigerator and microwave in each room that renders big savings and flexibility.
